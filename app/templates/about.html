Our Story

How LuckyNest came to be

LuckyNest was founded with a simple but powerful mission: to give paying guests a place that feels like home. What started as a single floor of well-maintained rooms has grown into a 50-room property spanning five floors, each designed to offer comfort, privacy, and convenience.

Our owner, Ms. Lucky, understood from the start that guests don't just need somewhere to sleep — they need a community, reliable services, and the peace of mind that comes from a well-managed building. That ethos is woven into everything we do, from our meal plans to our housekeeping schedules.

Today, LuckyNest is home to guests from all walks of life. Students, working professionals, and long-stay visitors choose us because they know exactly what they're getting: quality accommodation at honest prices, with the services they actually need.
